题解/Tutorial for HDU6602 - Longest Subarray

link Solution Let’s try to fix the right endpoint first and then find the longest subarray for each right endpoint. Let $pos_{x,i}$ be the index of the $i$-th occurrence of number $x$. Assume the current right endpoint is $r\in[0,n)$, for each $x\in[1,C]$ The left endpoint can’t fall in the interval $[pos_{x,m-k+1}+1,i]$ where $m$ is the occurrence of $x$ until $r$. This is because if left endpoint in that interval, the occurrence of $x$ would be larger than zero and smaller than $K$, which doesn’t satisfy the constrain.
